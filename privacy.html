Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2025

🔒 Short version: ResumeForge processes all resume data locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information or resume content. No account is required. Nothing you type into the builder is ever sent to our servers.

1. What data we collect

ResumeForge collects no personal data through the resume builder. All information you enter — your name, contact details, work history, education, and skills — is processed entirely within your browser and is never transmitted to ResumeForge's servers.

When you close your browser tab, your resume data is gone unless you saved a PDF locally.

2. Local storage

The following items may be stored in your browser's localStorage to improve your experience:

  • AI API keys you choose to save (for Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google Gemini) — stored locally, never transmitted to ResumeForge
  • Feedback prompt dismissal flags (fb_toast_seen, fb_exit_seen) — used to avoid showing prompts repeatedly

You can clear all localStorage data by clearing your browser's site data for thefreeresumebuilder.com at any time.

3. AI features

If you use the AI writing assistant, you provide your own API key from a third-party provider (Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google). When you generate content:

  • Your API key and prompt text are sent directly from your browser to that provider (e.g., api.anthropic.com)
  • ResumeForge's servers are not involved in this request in any way
  • Your usage is subject to the privacy policy of your chosen AI provider

We strongly recommend reviewing the privacy policies of Anthropic, OpenAI, and Google if you use their services.

4. Advertising

ResumeForge displays standard display advertisements and Infolinks placements. Advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads.

You can limit personalized advertising through your browser privacy settings and the opt-out tools provided by the relevant advertising partners.

5. Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns (page views, referrers, browser types). Cloudflare Web Analytics is privacy-focused and does not use cookies or fingerprinting. No individual user is tracked.

6. Cookies

ResumeForge itself does not require cookies for the resume builder. Advertising partners may set cookies for ad delivery or measurement. Cloudflare may set functional cookies as part of its CDN and security infrastructure.

7. Third-party services

ResumeForge loads the following third-party resources:

  • Google Fonts — for typography (Syne, IBM Plex Mono, DM Sans, etc.)
  • Cloudflare CDN — for jsPDF, html2canvas, and SortableJS libraries
  • Infolinks — for contextual advertising
  • Adsterra display units — for standard banner advertising

8. Children's privacy

ResumeForge is intended for users 16 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when changes were made. Continued use of ResumeForge after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Open an issue on GitHub.
