About ResumeForge

ResumeForge is a free resume builder that runs entirely in your browser. No account. No subscription. No data sent anywhere. Build a professional resume and download it as a PDF in minutes — completely free, forever.

Why we built this

The biggest resume builders on the internet — Resume.io, Zety, LiveCareer — charge $25–$30 per month. They let you build your resume for free, then paywall the PDF download. Job seekers who are already stressed about finding work shouldn't have to pay just to save their own document.

ResumeForge gives you professional templates, customization tools, and AI writing assistance at zero cost. The PDF download is and always will be free.

Privacy commitment

Your resume contains sensitive information — your employer history, personal contact details, skills, education. That data belongs to you. ResumeForge processes everything locally in your browser using JavaScript. When you close the tab, nothing persists on any server.

This is especially important for job seekers who are quietly searching while still employed. Nothing you type into ResumeForge is ever transmitted anywhere.

AI features use your own API key, which is stored only in your browser's localStorage and sent directly to your chosen AI provider (Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google). ResumeForge never sees or stores your API key.

How we keep the lights on

ResumeForge is supported by Google AdSense display advertising. You'll see standard display ads on the site. We don't sell your data, run trackers, or use dark patterns. Ads are the only revenue source — which means the tool itself stays genuinely free.
